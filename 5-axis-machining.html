About Fedele Autocore

Fedele Autocore stands at the forefront of advanced manufacturing, specialising in 5-axis machining technology to deliver precision components that meet the exacting standards of Australian industry. Our state-of-the-art manufacturing capabilities ensure every component we produce exceeds expectations for quality, precision, and reliability.

While our manufacturing expertise originates from international facilities, we maintain a strong commitment to serving the Australian market with components that meet local industry requirements and international quality standards. Our 5-axis machining technology enables us to create complex geometries and intricate designs that traditional machining methods simply cannot achieve.

We understand the unique challenges faced by Australian industries, from the harsh operating conditions in mining and agriculture to the stringent requirements of aerospace and defence applications. Every component we manufacture is designed to withstand these demanding environments while maintaining peak performance.

5 Axis Machining Services

Complex Geometry Machining

Our 5 axis capabilities allow us to machine complex parts in a single setup, reducing lead times and improving accuracy. Perfect for intricate aerospace components and defence applications requiring tight tolerances.

Multi-Material Expertise

We work with a wide range of materials including titanium alloys, aluminium, stainless steel, and exotic metals commonly used in Australian aerospace and defence industries.

Rapid Prototyping

Fast turnaround on prototype components allows Australian companies to accelerate their product development cycles and get to market faster with thoroughly tested designs.

Production Scalability

From single prototypes to high-volume production runs, our manufacturing systems scale to meet your needs while maintaining consistent quality across every component produced.

Quality Assurance

Comprehensive quality control processes including CMM inspection, surface finish analysis, and material certification ensure every component meets Australian industry standards.

Custom Tooling Solutions

Our engineering team develops custom tooling solutions optimised for your specific components, reducing machining time and improving surface finish quality.
