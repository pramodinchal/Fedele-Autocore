Precision CNC Machining Excellence

Leading the industry with advanced CNC machining capabilities across a comprehensive range of high-performance materials. From aerospace-grade alloys to heavy-duty industrial components, we deliver precision manufacturing solutions tailored to your exact specifications.

Material Categories

Carbon & Alloy Steels

  • Carbon Steel (1045, 4140) – Gears, shafts, structural components
  • Alloy Steel (4340, 8620) – Transmission parts, cylinders, rods
  • Chrome-Moly Steel (P11, P22) – High-temp piping applications
  • Carbon Steel (A105, A350) – Pressure vessels, flanges

Stainless Steel Grades

  • Standard Stainless (304, 316, 316L) – Exhaust systems, corrosion resistance
  • Precipitation Hardening (17-4 PH, 15-5 PH) – Aerospace valve components
  • Duplex Stainless (2205, 2507) – Superior corrosion resistance

Aluminum Alloys

  • 6061 & 6061-T6 – Lightweight housings, brackets
  • 7075 & 7075-T6 – Aircraft structures, wheels
  • 2024-T3 – Aerospace structural applications
  • A356 Casting Alloy – Engine blocks, complex castings

Cast Iron & Steel

  • Gray Cast Iron – Engine blocks, brake rotors
  • Ductile Cast Iron – Heavy-duty housings
  • Cast Steel – Structural components

Copper Alloys

  • Bronze Alloys – Bushings, bearings, wear plates
  • Brass Alloys – Decorative applications, wear components
  • Copper – Electrical components, exchangers

Specialty & Exotic Alloys

  • Hastelloy C-276 – Extreme corrosion resistance
  • 300M Steel – Ultra high-strength aerospace
  • Chrome-plated Steel – Wear-resistant piston rods
  • Weld Overlay Materials – Surface enhancement

Manufacturing Infrastructure & Capabilities

Our state-of-the-art manufacturing facilities combine advanced machinery, precision fabrication, and rigorous quality control systems to deliver exceptional aerospace components.

Machine Shop Capabilities

  • Precision Turning – High-accuracy cylindrical components
  • CNC Milling – Complex multi-axis machining
  • Boring & Tapping – Precision hole operations
  • Ring Rolling – Seamless ring production
  • Hot Marking – Permanent part identification
  • Laser Marking – Precise component traceability
  • Surface Treatments – Enhanced material properties
  • Heat Treatments – Optimized strength characteristics
  • Finishing Surface – Superior surface quality

Forming & Fabrication

  • Press Shop – Bending, Blanking, Swaging, Welding
  • Hot Forging – 0.5kg to 200kg capacity
  • Screw Press Forging – 0.1kg to 25kg capacity
  • Casting – SG Iron, CI, MG Casting
  • Investment Casting – Precision components

Quality Control Equipment

  • Coordinate Measuring Machine (CMM) – Dimensional accuracy
  • Spectrometer for Material Analysis – Chemical composition
  • Burble Projector – Profile inspection
  • Hardness Tester – Material property verification
  • Digital Vernier & Micrometers – Precision measurement
  • Height Gauge & Angle Projector – Geometric verification
  • Thread Gauge & Air Gauge – Threading accuracy

Quality Management Systems

  • Control Charts – Statistical process control
  • 8D Problem Solving – Systematic issue resolution
  • APQR (Advanced Product Quality Review) – Quality validation
  • FMEA (Failure Mode & Effects Analysis) – Risk assessment
  • PPAP (Production Part Approval Process) – Manufacturing validation
  • SPC (Statistical Process Control) – Process monitoring
  • MSA (Measurement System Analysis) – Measurement reliability
