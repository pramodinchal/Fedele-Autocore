Large Scale Production Capabilities Production Capabilities

Advanced CNC manufacturing solutions designed to meet Australia's most demanding industrial requirements with precision, efficiency, and uncompromising quality standards.

Large Scale Production Excellence

Fedele Autocore specializes in high-volume manufacturing across all our CNC capabilities, delivering consistent quality and precision for projects of any magnitude across Australia.

High-Volume CNC Machining

Our state-of-the-art CNC machining centers are optimized for large-scale production runs, ensuring consistent quality across thousands of components. From automotive parts to industrial machinery components, we maintain tight tolerances even in high-volume scenarios.

Multi-Axis Manufacturing

Our advanced multi-axis CNC systems enable complex geometries to be produced efficiently in large quantities. This capability reduces setup times and increases throughput while maintaining the precision required for critical applications.

Automated Production Lines

Integrated automation systems streamline our large-scale production processes, featuring robotic loading/unloading, automated quality inspection, and continuous operation capabilities to maximize productivity and minimize lead times.

Quality Assurance at Scale

Advanced quality control systems including coordinate measuring machines (CMM) and statistical process control ensure every component meets specifications, regardless of production volume. Real-time monitoring maintains consistency throughout large production runs.
