About Us

Our Company

Fedele Autocore Established In Year 2004. We Are One Of The Noted Exporters And Manufactured Of Precision Machine Parts, Forging & Casting. These Are Use In Oil And Gas Industry, Machines, Tractors, Value Industry And Hydraulic Industry.

We Are Exporting Material To Different Countries From Last 20 Years. Quality And Accuracy Of Every Single Component Are Inspected Live On Production Lines And Off Line By Quality Analyst After Well Defined Interval Of Time.
