Our CNC Machining Capabilities

At Fedele Autocore, our CNC machining capabilities are built to handle the most complex geometries and tightest tolerances. We combine advanced technology, high-speed tooling, and skilled craftsmanship to deliver precision-engineered parts for automotive, aerospace, medical, and defense industries.

Our modern facility is equipped with state-of-the-art 3-axis, 4-axis, and 5-axis CNC machining centers, as well as high-speed milling and precision turning machines. Every part we produce undergoes rigorous quality inspections to ensure consistent performance and reliability under demanding conditions.
