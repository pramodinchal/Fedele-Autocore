Fedele Autocore stands as Australia's premier precision machining specialist, delivering exceptional manufacturing solutions that combine cutting-edge technology with uncompromising quality standards. Our comprehensive machining capabilities serve diverse industries across the continent, from aerospace and medical devices to automotive and defense applications.

With state-of-the-art CNC equipment, skilled craftsmen, and rigorous quality control processes, we transform raw materials into precision-engineered components that exceed expectations. Our commitment to excellence, innovation, and customer satisfaction has established us as the trusted partner for Australia's most demanding manufacturing requirements.

Comprehensive Machining Services

Our advanced manufacturing facility offers a complete range of precision machining services, utilizing the latest CNC technology and manufacturing techniques to deliver components with exceptional accuracy, superior surface finishes, and reliable performance.

CNC Turning Services

Precision cylindrical machining operations including external and internal turning, threading, grooving, and complex multi-diameter components. Our advanced CNC lathes deliver exceptional accuracy and surface finish quality for shafts, bushings, pins, and custom rotational parts.

CNC Milling Services

Advanced multi-axis milling capabilities for complex geometries, precision pockets, intricate contours, and detailed features. From 3-axis to 5-axis simultaneous machining, we create components with superior dimensional accuracy and exceptional surface finishes.

Custom Precision Parts

Specialized manufacturing solutions for unique requirements, prototypes, and small to large production runs. Our engineering team works closely with clients to optimize designs for manufacturability while maintaining performance specifications.

Advanced Manufacturing Technologies

Fedele Autocore continuously invests in cutting-edge manufacturing technologies to maintain our position as Australia's leading precision machining specialist.

Multi-Axis CNC Machining

Our facility features advanced 3, 4, and 5-axis CNC machining centers capable of producing complex geometries in single setups. This technology reduces setup time, improves accuracy, and enables the creation of intricate parts that would be impossible with conventional machining methods.

High-Speed Machining (HSM)

High-speed machining capabilities enable rapid material removal while achieving superior surface finishes. This technology is particularly beneficial for aluminum aerospace components, thin-wall parts, and applications requiring minimal post-processing.

Hard Machining

Our hard machining capabilities allow us to machine materials up to 65 HRC, eliminating the need for EDM operations in many applications. This provides superior surface finishes and dimensional accuracy for tool and die components, molds, and wear-resistant parts.

Micro Machining

Precision micro-machining capabilities for small, intricate components requiring exceptional accuracy. Ideal for medical devices, electronics, and precision instruments where traditional machining methods are inadequate.

Quality Control & Inspection

Every component produced at Fedele Autocore undergoes rigorous quality control processes to ensure conformance to specifications and customer requirements:

Incoming Material Inspection

All raw materials are thoroughly inspected upon receipt, including material certification verification, dimensional checks, and surface quality assessment to ensure only the highest quality materials enter our production process.

In-Process Monitoring

Continuous monitoring during machining operations using statistical process control methods, ensuring dimensional accuracy and surface finish quality throughout production runs.

Final Inspection

Comprehensive final inspection using coordinate measuring machines (CMM), optical measurement systems, surface roughness testing, and visual inspection to verify all specifications are met.

Documentation & Traceability

Complete documentation packages including material certificates, inspection reports, and traceability records for critical applications requiring full documentation compliance.
