About Us

Our Company

Fedele Autocore was established in 2004. We are one of the noted exporters and manufacturers of precision machine parts, forging, and casting. Our products are widely used in the oil and gas industry, machines, tractors, valve industry, and hydraulic systems.

For the last 20+ years, we have been exporting to different countries. Every single component undergoes stringent quality checks both on production lines and offline by experienced analysts at regular intervals.
