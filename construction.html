About Construction Industry

Fedele Autocore delivers precision-engineered components that power the construction industry. From heavy machinery parts to structural fastening systems, our manufacturing expertise ensures durability and reliability in the toughest job sites. We produce critical components for excavators, cranes, concrete equipment, and material handling systems that keep your projects moving forward.

Our advanced CNC machining capabilities handle everything from hardened steel pins and bushings to custom hydraulic cylinder components and wear-resistant parts. We work with construction equipment manufacturers, contractors, and maintenance teams across Australia, delivering components that meet strict dimensional requirements and withstand demanding operational conditions including dust, vibration, and heavy loads.
