Defence CNC Machining Adelaide

Fedele Autocore delivers precision CNC machining solutions for defence and military applications. Specialized manufacturing with stringent quality control for critical defence components and aerospace parts.

Specialized Defence Manufacturing Services

Precision CNC Machining

Advanced 3, 4, and 5-axis CNC machining for complex defence components. High-precision manufacturing with tolerances meeting military specifications and defence industry standards.

Military Grade Components

Manufacturing of critical military components including weapon system parts, vehicle components, and tactical equipment with certified quality assurance processes.

Aerospace CNC Parts

Specialized aerospace component manufacturing for defence aircraft, UAVs, and military aviation systems with AS9100 quality management compliance.

Prototype Development

Rapid prototyping and development services for defence contractors and military equipment manufacturers with quick turnaround times and design optimization.

Security Cleared Operations

Secure manufacturing environment with cleared personnel for sensitive defence projects requiring confidentiality and specialized handling protocols.

Quality Assurance

Comprehensive quality control with advanced inspection equipment, material traceability, and compliance with defence industry quality standards and certifications.

Advanced Manufacturing Capabilities

Multi-Axis CNC Machining

3, 4, and 5-axis CNC centers for complex geometries and tight tolerances required in defence applications.

Material Expertise

Aluminum alloys, titanium, stainless steel, inconel, and specialized defence-grade materials machining capabilities.

Precision Turning

High-precision CNC turning centers for shafts, pins, and cylindrical components with micron-level accuracy.

Complex Milling

Advanced milling operations for intricate defence components including housings, brackets, and structural elements.

Surface Treatments

Anodizing, passivation, and specialized coatings for enhanced durability and corrosion resistance.

Inspection Services

CMM inspection, dimensional analysis, and material certification for complete quality documentation.
